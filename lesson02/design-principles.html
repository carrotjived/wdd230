Visual Hierarchy

Netflix

netflix.com

Netflix is a streaming website that let's user first notice a random playing show that has size bigger than the rest of the content, then below that big screen are smaller pictures that shows what other videos are avaialable to be watched, and finally at the last part are smaller than previous pictures that show other contents available to watch.

Rule of Thirds

Amazon

powermaccenter.com

Powermac webpage uses rule of thirds principle by organizing their elements in an imaginary nine by nine grid. Their icon on their top left corner, search bar at the top center, and user tools on their top right corner. A gallery of products consumes the center most row of the grid and the last row is compromised by the rest of their content for sale.

Repetition

Nike

www.nike.com/ph

Nike used uniform style, color, size to each of their headings and content throughout all the webpage. The visual images are uniform as well as well as their whitespaces and gaps to each other.
