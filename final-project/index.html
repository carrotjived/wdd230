About Cozumal Island

Cozumel Mexico is the largest inhabited island in Mexico and the oldest in the Caribbean group.

Cozumel is located 12 miles off the eastern coast of the mainland (Yucatan Peninsula). The limestone plateau that forms the base of the island is 34 miles long (north to south) and 11 miles wide (east to west).
