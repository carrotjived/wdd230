2024 BOSS IRONMAN CHALLENGE

Witness the endurance event of BOSS ('BMW Owners' Society of Safe Riders') as they make a stop in Plaza Burgos. Participants are challenged to drive a route spanning at least 1,200 kilometers in under 24 hours to be named a "Finisher".

Kannawidan Festival

Join us in celebrating the 32nd Kannawidan Festival. A week long of celebration of Parades, Activities, and Music Festivals.

Binatbayan Festival

Binatbatan Festival, also known as Viva Vigan Festival of the Arts, was created to promote awareness of the City's cultural heritage and centered on the loom weaving industry of the City, Thus transforms favorable destination images and loyalty among visitors and tourist attending the festival
