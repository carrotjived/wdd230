Site Name: One Ilocos Chamber of Commerce

This name represents the vision of the province where this chamber conducts their businesses.

Site Purpose

The One Ilocos Chamber of Commerce website is the information go-to for current and interested future members of the chamber of upcoming activities, important announcements etc..This site has the purpose of promoting businesses by posting online advertisement at the same time contact channel amongst members of the chamber.

Scenarios

  • How can I contact the chamber members?
  • What are the upcoming projects of the chamber for this year that can increase business opportunities and networking?
  • What are the benefits of joining this chamber?
